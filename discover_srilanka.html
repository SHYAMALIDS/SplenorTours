Discover Sri Lanka

Detailed Itinerary


Day 1: Airport/Negombo

On arrival at Colombo International Airport you will be met by a Splenor Tours Representative and transferred to your hotel in Negombo. Check in at your hotel â€“ relax and unwind after your journey. Evening City & shopping tour in Colombo.

Day 2: Colombo/Pinnawala /kandy

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to Kandy. En route visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage which houses abounded and wounded elephants.
Proceed to Kandy in the afternoon. Visit the temple of tooth Relic of Lord Buddha. Witness a Cultural Dance Show in the evening.
Overnight stay in Kandy.

Day 3: Kandy/ Nuwara Eliya

Breakfast at the hotel. Visit The Royal Botanical Garden one of the largest in Asia.
Leave to Peradeniya Railway station, and board the train and leave to Nuwara Eliya. Enjoy the picturesque journey from your observation carriage, the train snaking its way through the mountains through the tea country. Wander through the trails of British colonial era with buildings ranging from Georgian to Queen Anne , Golf course with 18 holes, the hill club of the English Tea Planters.

Day 04:Nuwara Eliya / Horton Planes(approx:3 hrs)/ Nuwara Eliya

After breakfast do an excursion to . (Nuwara Eliya / Horton Plains- Approx: 1 hr one way- about 3 hrs in the park)
Horton Plains is home for a wide variety of Flora and species of Mammals such as Deer, Elk, Giant Squirrel, Wild Boar, Porcupine, Wild Bear and Leopard. For Bird Enthusiasts there are 87 species including many migratory birds.
Return to hotel in the afternoon.

Day 5: Nuwara Eliya/Galle

Breakfast at the hotel. Transfer to Galle. En route visit a Tea Factory and a Tea Plantation where you could see the process of manufacturing of tea while enjoying a world famous Ceylon Tea. You could also experience the plucking of tea buds as well. The journey to Galle is full of beautiful sceneries with mild weather condition.
Evening Check in your hotel at Galle.

Day 6: Galle / Airport (approx: 4 hrs 30 min)

After breakfast indulge in an Ayurveda Treatment. Visit Dutch Fort. Galle Fort was built first in 1588 by the Portuguese, then extensively fortified by the Dutch during the 17th century from 1649 onwards. It is a World Heritage Site, which even after more than 423 years is full of live with cafÃ©s, homes, galleries and boutiques. (UNESCO World Heritage)
Transfer to the airport in time for your departure
